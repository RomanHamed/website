  • Mini Hockey Table

Our Semester-long project for my Advanced Engineering Honors class was to build a miniature version of an Air Hockey table. It would be powered by the vacuum cleaners that you have in your house. I worked along with four of my classmates only starting with a very basic outer frame. I specifically worked on soldering the electronics, designing the pucks, and building the goals.
